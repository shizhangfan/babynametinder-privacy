Privacy Policy

Baby Name Tinder

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Plain-language summary

Baby Name Tinder helps couples discover and match on baby names together. We collect only what is needed to make the app work. We do not sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with anyone except the third-party services listed below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask for your name, email address, or any contact information to use the app. Specifically, we collect:

  • Anonymous user ID — automatically assigned by Firebase when you first open the app. No email or password is required.
  • Display name — the first name you optionally enter during onboarding, visible only to your partner.
  • Swiped name data — which baby names you liked or skipped, stored to power the matching feature.
  • Family circle membership — the invite code and member list of the circle you create or join with your partner.
  • Daily swipe count & last swipe date — used to enforce free-tier limits and reset them daily.
  • Subscription status — whether you are on the free or lifetime premium plan, stored after a purchase is verified.
  • AI preferences — optional style preferences (e.g., name origin, style) you enter to personalise AI-generated name suggestions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Sync your liked names with your partner's in real time so matches can be detected.
  • Enforce and reset daily swipe limits for free users.
  • Restore premium access after a verified purchase.
  • Generate personalised name suggestions based on your stated preferences.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Baby Name Tinder uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication) — stores user data and syncs it between partners in real time. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — processes in-app purchases. Apple handles all payment data; we never see your payment information. Apple Privacy Policy
  • AI name suggestion providers — if you use the AI suggestions feature, your stated name preferences (style, origin) are sent to a third-party AI API to generate recommendations. No personal identifying information is included in these requests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • With your partner — your display name and matched names are visible to members of the same family circle.
  • With Firebase — as described above, to store and sync app data.
  • If required by law — we may disclose information if required by a valid legal process.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored in Firebase Firestore for as long as your anonymous account exists. To delete all your data:

  • Delete your family circle (if you are the admin) from the Profile tab inside the app.
  • Contact us at the email below and we will manually delete your Firebase records within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Baby Name Tinder is designed for use by adults who are expecting parents. The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Firebase with access controls that prevent any user from reading another user's data outside of their linked family circle.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
